About me

Hey, I'm Thomas!

Originally from the city of Vitória in Brazil, I've now lived in Los Angeles for most of my life. My family and I moved into the suburb of Palos Verdes, where I attended Palos Verdes High School. Afterwards, I went into the heart of LA to study Business Administration, with emphasis in Cinematic Arts and a Specialization in Entrepreneurship at the USC Marshall School of Business.

Most of my work since has been in advertising and digital marketing. I'm currently refining my web-development abilities with personal projects, as well as completing the Project Management course at Calbright College, as those are new directions I'd like to take my career into - and otherwise, I think it's a great knowledge base to have in my tool belt regardless.

At the moment, my primary focus is to bring my skillset into the Esports, Live-Streaming, and Gaming scenes.
